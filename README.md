# Privacy Policy for Calorie Tracker

**Effective Date:** April 26, 2026

## 1. Overview
This app ("Calorie Tracker") is designed to help users track daily calorie intake and related nutritional information. Your privacy is important, and this app is built to minimize data collection.

---

## 2. Information We Collect
The app may store the following information **locally on your device**:

- Food entries (meals, calories, notes)
- Daily logs and history
- Optional user-entered data (weight, goals, etc.)

We **do not collect personally identifiable information** such as:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information
All data is used solely to:

- Display your calorie tracking history
- Provide insights and summaries (daily/weekly totals)
- Improve your personal tracking experience

---

## 4. Data Storage
- All data is stored **locally on your device**
- We do **not upload your data to any servers**
- We do **not share your data with third parties**

---

## 5. Permissions
The app may request minimal permissions required for functionality.

For example:
- Storage access (if backup/export features are used)

We do **not access sensitive data** like contacts, messages, or location.

---

## 6. Data Security
Since your data stays on your device, its security depends on your device’s protection (PIN, password, biometrics, etc.).

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy
This app is not specifically directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy
This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
